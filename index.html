HR insights

Turnover is a supervision-quality story

Top supervisors correlate with faster new-hire ramp. Fund retention bonuses and clone the best onboarding playbook before losing them at 12%/mo churn.

Concert-weekend OT degrades reliability

Bar and maintenance lost reliability points during peak OT — fatigue, not character. Build a backup bench and cap consecutive OT shifts.
